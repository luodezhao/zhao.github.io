Yang Liu 劉 洋

I am a Senior Research Fellow of the Leverhulme Centre for the Future of Intelligence and the Faculty of Philosophy at the University of Cambridge. Prior to Cambridge, I did my PhD in Philosophy at Columbia University in the City of New York.

My research interests include Logic, Probability, Decision Theory, and Artificial Intelligence. My research is currently supported by the Leverhulme Trust and the Isaac Newton Trust.
